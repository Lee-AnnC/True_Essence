Harvesting Your Plants

Feb 17, 2021

Knowing when to harvest your plants is important. It’s always best to harvest plants in the time just before flowering and when half the flowers are open.
There are exceptions. Lavender is best harvested when the flowers have already opened and withered. Rosemary is best harvested in full bloom.
